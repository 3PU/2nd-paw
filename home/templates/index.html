Who are we?

2ND PAWS is a non-profit organization, closely working together with all major cat & dog shelters in the Stockholm area. We are providing pet owners with an online meeting portal to donate/exchange pet clothes, toys and supplies that they no longer need/use. All profits generated on our website go directly to the cat and dog shelters in Stockholm, supporting their fantastic work.

Have you ever...

...experienced this? You buy a new toy, full of excitement to see your pet play with it. But once you present it, you're left with an empty look on your pets face only to see your furry friend wanders off and start playing with some random thing in your house? Now you're sitting there with a toy that you have no use for as your pet was completely uninterested?

Rest assured, this is a very common experience for many pet owners and we all have faced this akward situation many times. At 2nd Paws we offer you the opportunity to donate such a product so it can be of use to other pet owners and pets whilst having a positive impact by supporting Stockholms cat and dog shelters in their amazing job.

Positive Impact

Donating products via our service instead of throwing them away...

  • Protects the environment!
  • Helps to fund and support the work of multiple cat/dog shelters around Stockholm!
  • Gives the product a second life and brings happiness to other pet owners and pets!

Simply put, a easy second hand service for animal products, hence our slogan"From paw to paw".

For more information about our service and how it works, please visit the FAQ page.
